Okay so first thing is first. What is HTML & CSS?HTML stands for Hyper Text Markup Language. It is used to create and structure the content on webpages. Think of this as the structure of a house. It gives the house its shape and tells you where the rooms/windows/doors go. CSS stands for Cascading Style Sheets. It is used to style the content on webpages. Think of this as the paint and decorations of a house. It givesthe house its color and style.

Okay! Yay you were able to make a boiler plate for html! Now what? The hell am I supposed to do with this? Well, dont panic just yet! Now we are going to go into the basics of HTML.

lets talk about the structure of code.college. Everything here on the website is inside of our HTML boilerplate. The first thing you see is the header. The header is where you put the title of your webpage. The title is what shows up on the tab of your browser. The next thing you see is the body. The body is where you put all the content of your webpage. This is where you put all the text, images, and videos that you want to show up on your webpage. The body is like the main part of your webpage. It is where all the action happens.

Headers are represented by the <h1> to <h6> tags. The <h1> tag is the largest header and the</h6> tag is the smallest header. Paragraphs are represented by the <p> tag and is used to create a new paragraph. You may also notice that there are opening and closing tags. The opening tag just shows where to start and end an element. The closing tag is the same as the opening tag but with a / before the element name.

Now that you know a little bit about headers and paragraphs lets try to make some headers and paragraphs to get the crown!

Okay yay! We see what headers are now and we can go ahead and learn about my next favorite HTML concepts. Divs and Spans! Divs and Spans are used to group elements together. Think of them as containers. Divs are block containers like a big box that you can use to put stuff inside it. Spans is like a small sticker that you can put on something. Its not a big box like a div its just for something you want to highlight.
