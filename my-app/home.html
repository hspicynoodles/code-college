Coding Applications

code.college teaches and applies our skills on small projects, allowing you to dive deeper into your understanding of the material. We have a variety of projects that you can work on and learn from. With these projects, you can learn how to apply your skills in a real-world setting while learning the basics.

Enjoy your journey! If you have comments, suggestions, or feedback, please email me at hrmacias@asu.edu

Getting Started - Learn the Basics

The material on code.college is split into different "forts." Each fort will cover a topic, and with that information, you will need to hard-code your way to get the crown.

Each fort will have a different topic and a set of challenges. The challenges will be based on the topic of the fort and will require you to use the information you've learned to solve them.

Note: If you run into problems with concepts and challenges, you can get help on our Discord server!
